AuraGlow Electronics - AuraGlow Pro Projector User Manual

1. Package Contents
- AuraGlow Pro Projector
- Remote Control (requires 2 AAA batteries)
- Power Adapter
- HDMI Cable
- Quick Start Guide

2. Setting Up Your Projector
- Connect the Power Adapter to the projector and plug it into a power outlet.
- Press the Power Button on top of the projector or the remote.
- Connect your device (computer, console, streaming stick) via the HDMI port.
- Adjust the Focus Ring located near the lens until the image is clear.
- Use the Keystone Correction slider on the side to align the image angles.

3. Connecting to Wi-Fi
- Go to Settings > Network > Wi-Fi.
- Select your wireless network and enter the password.
- A green checkmark will appear when connected.

4. Troubleshooting
- No Image: Ensure cables are plugged in and the lens cap is removed. Check if the source device is powered on.
- Blurry Image: Rotate the Focus Ring. Ensure the projector is at least 1.5 meters away from the wall.
- Projector Overheating: Keep the ventilation vents clear. If the indicator light flashes red, turn off the device and let it cool for 30 minutes.
- Wi-Fi Disconnection: Restart the projector and your router. Ensure the projector is within range of the router.
- Firmware Updates: Go to Settings > System > Update to download the latest system software.

AuraGlow Electronics - Refund & Return Policy

1. Return Window
Customers can return any AuraGlow hardware product within 30 days of the purchase date for a full refund or exchange. The item must be in its original packaging and in like-new condition.

2. Restocking Fee
A 10% restocking fee applies to open-box returns of premium items (such as the AuraGlow Pro Projector).

3. Non-Returnable Items
- Digital software downloads and license keys.
- Gift cards.
- Clearance items marked "Final Sale".

4. Refund Process
Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of the status. Approved refunds will be processed back to the original method of payment within 5 to 7 business days.

5. How to Initiate a Return
Visit our Returns Portal at auraglow.com/returns or contact billing support at billing@auraglow.com.

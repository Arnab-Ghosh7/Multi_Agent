AuraGlow Electronics - Shipping Policy

1. Shipping Rates & Delivery Times
- Standard Shipping (3-5 business days): Free for orders over $50, otherwise $4.99.
- Express Shipping (1-2 business days): $14.99 flat rate.
- International Shipping (7-14 business days): Calculated at checkout (varies by destination).

2. Order Processing
Orders are processed within 24 hours on business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the next business day.

3. Tracking Numbers
You will receive a shipment confirmation email with a tracking number as soon as your order leaves our warehouse.

4. Lost or Damaged Packages
If your package is lost or arrives damaged, please contact support immediately at support@auraglow.com with your order number. Claims must be submitted within 14 days of delivery.

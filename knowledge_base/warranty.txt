AuraGlow Electronics - Warranty Information

1. Warranty Period
All hardware products manufactured by AuraGlow Electronics come with a one-year (12 months) limited manufacturer warranty from the date of retail purchase.

2. Coverage Details
This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal, residential use.

3. Exclusions
This warranty does NOT cover:
- Damage resulting from accidents, abuse, misuse, or alteration.
- Liquid damage or immersion.
- Repairs attempted by unauthorized personnel.
- Normal wear and tear (e.g., cosmetic scratches).

4. Warranty Service
To claim warranty service, you must provide proof of purchase. We will, at our discretion, either repair the product using new or refurbished parts, replace the product with an equivalent model, or refund the purchase price. Contact warranty@auraglow.com to initiate a claim.
